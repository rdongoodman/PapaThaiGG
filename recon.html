Sector Alpha: Ao Nang (Home Base)

Ao Nang is the primary deployment hub for the Krabi province. For expats and gamers, it offers the best balance of infrastructure and island access. You'll find high-speed fiber, international supplies, and the main pier for longtail boat sorties.

Sector Bravo: Railay Peninsula

Accessible only by sea, Railay is a world-class climbing destination. It consists of four distinct zones. Railay West is your primary target for sunsets, while Phra Nang Beach holds the sacred Princess Cave. It is a no-car zoneâ€”foot traffic only.

Operational Intel: Island Sorties

The 4-Island Tour is a must for any new recruit. This includes Poda Island, Chicken Island, and the sandbar at Koh Tup. For a cleaner "render" and better snorkeling, head further out to the Hong Islands or the Phi Phi Sector.
